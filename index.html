05 / Studio

A small team in close focus.

Slingshot Studios is a multidisciplinary production studio working across film, music, and actor-focused content.

Independent by choice. Fewer projects means more attention to the details that matter — framing, rhythm, sound. The things that make a film worth rewatching.

Based in Paris. Founded in 2025. We take on a limited number of projects each year, working closely with directors, actors, and musicians.

Four pillars: Film production, Actor services, Post and Sound, and Design. All under one roof, by the same people.
