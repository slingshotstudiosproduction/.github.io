03 / Studio

A small team, working in close focus.

Slingshot Studios is a multidisciplinary production studio working across film, music, and actor-focused content. Built in Europe, made for the screen.

We're independent by choice. Smaller projects let us stay involved in the detail — framing, sound, rhythm — the things that make a film feel alive on repeat viewing.

We collaborate with directors, actors, and musicians across Paris, Berlin, and Milan, and take on a limited number of commissioned projects each year.

The studio is organised around four pillars: Film production, Actor services, Post & Sound, and Design — all under one roof, by the same people.
